Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 5, 2026

1. Data Collection

At PetCare AI, we take your privacy seriously. We collect minimal personal data necessary to provide you with the best possible service. This includes your account details (name, email), pet profiles (photos, health logs), and location data (if you choose to use our Missing Pet Community Map).

2. Use of Machine Learning

When you upload images of your pets for breed identification or health screening, these images are processed by our backend AI models. We do not sell your images or personal data to third parties. Data may be used in an anonymized, aggregated format to further improve our machine learning models.

3. Location Data

Our "Missing Pets" and "Locate Me" features utilize your device's GPS and background location services strictly to place accurate markers on the community map. This location data is stored securely and is only made public when you explicitly report a pet as missing.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures including database encryption, secure authentication (HTTPS), and restricted access controls to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and store certain functional information, such as your authentication session and basic UI preferences. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies, but some parts of our service may not function properly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. You can manage your pet profiles directly from your dashboard. To request complete account deletion, please contact our support team.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
